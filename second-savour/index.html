Why “Second Savour”?

Every year, thousands of kilograms of perfectly good citrus are tossed aside for being too ugly, too small, or too imperfect to sell. We think that's bananas (well, oranges). Second Savour rescues that fruit and transforms it into delicious treats you can feel good about eating.
