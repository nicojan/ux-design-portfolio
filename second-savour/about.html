Giving fruit a second life.

Second Savour started with a simple question: why does so much good fruit go to waste?

Image Placeholder

Every year, farms and distributors throw away tons of fruit that doesn't meet cosmetic standards. It's too small. Too spotty. The wrong shade of orange. None of that affects the taste, but it keeps perfectly good produce out of grocery stores and into landfills.

We started Second Savour to change that. We partner with local farms and food suppliers across BC to rescue fruit that would otherwise be wasted. Then we turn it into something worth eating.

Image Placeholder

Our treats use the whole fruit. Peel, pulp, everything. No part gets left behind. That means less waste, more flavour, and snacks you can feel good about.

Image Placeholder

The name says it all. Every piece of fruit we use gets a second chance to be savoured.
