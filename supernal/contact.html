About This Project

Supernal is a personal project about the long human habit of mapping the unmappable. It collects stories from the history of cosmography, sacred cartography, and celestial observation, moments when people tried to give shape to things that don't have edges.

All images come from public domain collections. The project draws on material from the Public Domain Review, the Internet Archive, Cornell University Library, and the British Library.
