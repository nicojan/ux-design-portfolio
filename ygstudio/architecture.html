System Architecture Proposal

Studio Operations Platform

A unified system that reduces operational drag by automating data handoffs, content production, and reporting — while keeping humans in the decision seat.

1

Lark stays home

All data lives in Lark Bases. Staff change nothing about how they work. The system listens and acts around them.

2

AI works silently

A Mac Mini runs automation 24/7 — syncing data, generating content, compiling reports. No cloud dependency for routine tasks.

3

Humans decide

Nothing posts, sends, or changes without staff approval. AI produces and recommends. People approve and deliver.
